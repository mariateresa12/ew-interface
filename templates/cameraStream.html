Gimbal Control

Each click moves the gimbal 10° in the indicated direction. Up/Down tilts the gimbal pitch joint; Left/Right pans the gimbal yaw joint. Click events publish a geometry_msgs/msg/Vector3 to the /gimbal/cmd ROS 2 topic. The on-board bridge accumulates the target and publishes a trajectory_msgs/msg/JointTrajectory to /set_joint_trajectory, which the simulator's libgazebo_ros_joint_pose_trajectory plugin applies directly to the gimbal joints.
